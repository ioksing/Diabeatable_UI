About Us

While diabetes is a well-studied and manageable chronic disease with many available treatments, we found a lack of existing technology supporting the management of a patient's lifestyle. With chronic diseases like diabetes, lifestyle changes and management are critical in staying healthy. Our idea involves designing technology that helps with managing blood sugar levels of diabetic patients on a continual basis.
